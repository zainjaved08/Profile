About

Evidence-based care, delivered patiently.

Zain Javed holds a Bachelor of Dietetics and Nutritional Sciences from the University of Sialkot and has spent the past three years inside hospital and clinical settings translating lab results and physician notes into food plans patients can actually follow.

His practice is built on the belief that nutrition counseling fails when it's generic. A diabetic plan and a PCOS plan are not interchangeable, and a plan that ignores what's actually available in a patient's kitchen won't survive past week one. Every protocol he writes starts from the patient's diagnosis, current medication, and daily routine.

Before dietetics, Zain spent years in administrative and clinical-support roles — work that shaped a habit of precise record-keeping and clear, calm communication that now carries directly into how he runs patient files and follow-up.
